1. Who We Are

Deep Delta Sleep is a sleep audio brand operated by Slow River Digital. Our primary platform is the YouTube channel @deep_delta_sleep. This website (deepdeltasleep.github.io) serves as our official online home.

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: slowriverdigital@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors. There are no forms, user accounts, comment sections, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels on this site.

We do not use cookies or any local storage mechanisms on this website.

Deep Delta Sleep publishes content on the following third-party platforms. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs how data is collected when you interact with our content there:

  • YouTube — Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok — TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Pinterest — Pinterest Privacy Policy

When you watch, like, comment on, or share our content on those platforms, data collection is governed by those platforms' terms — not by Deep Delta Sleep or Slow River Digital.

4. Pinterest API Use

Deep Delta Sleep uses the Pinterest API to publish Pins programmatically to our official Pinterest account. This application:

  • Does not collect, store, or sell any Pinterest user data
  • Only accesses our own Pinterest account to create and manage Pins
  • Does not read or store data from other Pinterest users
  • Does not request access to users' boards, pins, or personal information

Our Pinterest API integration is used solely for content publishing automation — posting sleep soundscape images and links to our YouTube videos.

5. YouTube API Use

Deep Delta Sleep uses the YouTube Data API and YouTube Analytics API to:

  • Upload videos to our own YouTube channel
  • Retrieve analytics data for our own channel only
  • Manage playlists and video metadata on our own channel

Our use of YouTube APIs is governed by YouTube API Services Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store data from other YouTube users.

6. Email Contact

7. Children's Privacy

This website and associated content are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
